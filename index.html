The story of Mugen Mugen (無限, Infinity) is one of the main male protagonists of Samurai Champloo along with Jin. Both Mugen and Jin accompany Fuu in her search for "the samurai who smells of sunflowers." Mugen serves as a foil to Jin in terms of personality and disposition. His fighting style is characterized as being reckless and erratic.

The Past of Mugen and the Ryukyu Islands Mugen was born in a penal colony on the Ryukyu Islands which in the Edo period was a separate kingdom with close ties to the Satsuma domain of Japan. His name means without limitation, as the components of it are the characters for "without" and "limitation." Incidentally

In the 19th century, the Ryūkyū Archipelago was annexed by Japan and became a prefecture of the country. During World War II, the Ryūkyū Archipelago was the scene of intense fighting and suffered heavy bombing by Allied forces. Since the end of the war, the Ryūkyū Archipelago has experienced rapid economic growth and has become a popular tourist destination. Today, the Ryūkyū Archipelago is also known for its unique culture and nature preservation.
